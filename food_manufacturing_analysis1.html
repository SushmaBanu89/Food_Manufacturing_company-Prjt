#Project Title:¶

Data Analysis & Forecasting to Improve Efficiency in a Food Manufacturing Company

🏭 Business Problem:

The food manufacturing company faced challenges including:

  1. High scrap/waste in certain product lines.

  2. Unbalanced production vs. demand, leading to overproduction and stockpiling.

  3. Significant downtime during specific shifts, reducing overall plant efficiency.

  4. Lack of demand forecasting, causing poor inventory planning.

    Project Objectives:

Identify operational inefficiencies across production units.

Analyze scrap rates and downtime patterns.

Recommend shift-level and product-level optimizations.

Build a predictive model to forecast future demand.

#🔹 Step 4: Forecast Next 15 Days

forecast = model_fit.forecast(steps=15)
forecast.index = pd.date_range(demand_df.index[-1] + pd.Timedelta(days=1), periods=15)

# Plot forecast
